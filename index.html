About Margaret

Margaret Berg is a passionate photographer who finds joy in capturing the beauty of the world through her lens. She has been honing her craft for over a decade, constantly seeking new perspectives and techniques to enhance her photography.

What Margaret loves most about photography is its ability to freeze moments in time, allowing her to relive memories and share them with others. Whether it's a stunning landscape or a candid portrait, Margaret strives to evoke emotion and tell stories through her photos.

In her free time, Margaret enjoys exploring nature trails with her camera in hand, seeking out the perfect shot. She also finds inspiration in everyday life, finding beauty in the mundane and extraordinary alike.

Most of the stunning artwork she creates is made with nature and with limetless creation.
